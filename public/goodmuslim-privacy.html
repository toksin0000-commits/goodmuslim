Privacy Policy for Good Muslim

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Welcome to Good Muslim. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • IP Address: When you post a wish or report content, we temporarily store your IP address to enforce our daily limit (one wish per day) and prevent abuse.
  • Language Preference: Your selected language (Arabic, English, Urdu) is stored to provide you with the correct interface.
  • Location Data: When you use the mosque finder feature, we request your location to show nearby mosques. This data is processed locally on your device and sent only to OpenStreetMap API to fetch mosque data. We do not store your location on our servers.
  • Wish Content: The wishes you post are stored in our database along with the date and time of posting.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service (displaying wishes, Quran, mosque locations)
  • To enforce our daily posting limit (one wish per IP address per day)
  • To moderate content (reported wishes are reviewed by administrators)
  • To improve our application based on usage patterns

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Our database provider where wishes, reports, and admin data are stored. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • OpenStreetMap: Used to fetch mosque locations based on your coordinates. OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy
  • Vercel: Hosting platform for our application. Vercel Privacy Policy
  • ipify.org: Used to detect your public IP address for rate limiting purposes. ipify Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your wishes and associated data
  • Request information about what data we hold about you
  • Opt out of any future data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: toksin0000@gmail.com

6. Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
